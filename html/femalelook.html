Winter Blues

"I love how easy this girl's style is. Her look is simple but versatile. I would wear this morning to night."

Head-to-toe monochromatic looks liven up heavy winter wardrobes when done in bright primary hues.

This trend has been going strong for a few seasons now, and doesn't seem to be fading anytime soon -- most likely because of the pure ease the look affords. The look is flattering on anyone. It's a lesson in proportions, and if you can master that, you can be blue (or red, or yellow, or black) all over.

Gone are the days when wearing one shade is no-no. Break this fashion rule by pairing a sweeping coat with tailored trousers and a elongating turtleneck to avoid looking like a blueberry. When in doubt, slim cuts can keep the trend from looking overwhelming, even on a petite frame.

There are no limitations to this trend -- match several shades of blue or patterns with the same color scheme if one solid color proves too intimdating.
