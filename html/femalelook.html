Spring Hues

Head-to-toe pattern brings life to transitional spring wardrobes when done in neon and neutral hues.

Print mixing is a trend that has been going strong for a few seasons now, and doesn't seem to be fading anytime soon -- most likely because of the versitility and artful look it affords. The look is difficult to wear but if you can master it, you will reign style supreme.

Pattern play is a lesson in proportion. Gone are the days when wearing clashing colors and prints is no-no. Break this fashion rule by pairing camo with stripes, neons with neutrals. Layer smartly and pair similar color stories together to unite all the pieces. When in doubt, slim cuts and lighter fabrics can keep the trend from looking overwhelming, even on a petite frame.

There are no limitations to this trend -- match patterns with the same color scheme together if going all the way proves too intimdating.
